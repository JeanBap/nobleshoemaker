The Philosophy

A journey into the soul of a great shoe

For years, The Noble Shoe has championed the belief that a great shoe is an investment in self — a statement of character wrought from the finest leathers, the most considered construction, and generations of accumulated knowledge. Now, for the first time, we are opening the doors.

This is not a holiday. It is an immersion. You will sit with the masters. You will cut, last, and stitch alongside cordwainers whose craft has been refined over centuries. You will understand, at last, why a Goodyear-welted shoe commands such devotion — because you will have built one with your own hands.

And in the evenings, you will eat and drink as Italy demands: with unhurried pleasure, in the company of people who care deeply about beautiful things.

Kostas Mandilaris Founder, The Noble Shoe

The Masters
You Will Meet

These are not demonstrations staged for tourists. These are private, working ateliers — and you will be a guest of their craft, not a spectator of it.

01
Naples, Italy
Paolo Scafora

One of the most celebrated bespoke cordwainers in the world. His shoes are worn by heads of state, connoisseurs, and those fortunate enough to know where to look. A full day in his atelier is extraordinarily rare.

02
Florence, Italy
La Callisto Atelier

Founded by Kostas himself in 2024, La Callisto produces handmade leather accessories of the highest order in the heart of Florence. An intimate workshop where you will understand leather as a material — not a product.

03
Tuscany
A Historic Tannery

Vegetable tanning is one of the oldest and most demanding crafts in Italian leather-making. At this private tannery — operating since the Middle Ages — you will see how the finest raw hides become the leather you hold in such regard.
